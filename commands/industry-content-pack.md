Build a complete content pack for the industry vertical specified in $ARGUMENTS.

## STEP 1: Parse Input
Extract the target industry vertical and any sub-vertical or use case focus from $ARGUMENTS.

## STEP 2: Mine Engagement Sources
Search all engagement sources for stories involving companies or contacts in this industry:
- HubSpot: deals and contacts tagged with industry or vertical
- Granola: meeting notes referencing industry-specific challenges
- Google Drive: project documents for clients in this vertical
- Slack: channels and threads related to industry engagements

Also note any industry-specific content Axelerant has already published (check axelerant.com/blog and axelerant.com/success-stories for reference).

## STEP 3: External Research Layer
For the specified industry, gather:
- Current digital transformation trends and pressures
- Technology adoption patterns (what platforms are being adopted or replaced?)
- Key industry reports or analyst research relevant to digital (Gartner, Forrester, IDC, industry associations)
- Regulatory or compliance changes affecting technology decisions
- Major challenges specific to this vertical's digital operations

## STEP 4: Build the Industry Content Pack

**A. Industry Overview Brief** (400-500 words)
A practitioner's summary of the digital landscape in this industry:
- Where the industry is in its digital maturity
- What problems are systemic and unsolved
- Where Axelerant's capabilities map to industry needs
- Key personas and what they care about

**B. Proof Points from Axelerant Engagements**
For each relevant engagement story found in Step 2:
- 150-200 word proof point: challenge → approach → outcome
- Client descriptor (anonymized): "a large professional association", "a mid-market healthcare nonprofit", etc.
- Axelerant service scope and platform used

**C. Industry Blog Brief** (if sufficient material exists for Tier 1)
- 3 headline options for an industry-focused thought leadership blog
- Recommended angle: external research + Axelerant engagement proof
- Suggested structure with section headings
- Key claims to make and support with evidence

**D. Industry LinkedIn Content Calendar** (4 weeks of posts)
Produce 4 LinkedIn post concepts for this vertical:
- Post 1: Industry trend or challenge (thought leadership)
- Post 2: Proof point from an engagement (use-case)
- Post 3: Platform insight relevant to this industry
- Post 4: Call to conversation (question/opinion post for engagement)

**E. ABM Outreach Narrative** (for Industry Play campaigns)
200-250 words written for BDR/sales use with named accounts in this vertical:
- "We've been working with organizations like yours to solve [X]..."
- Reference to specific outcome (anonymized)
- Soft CTA appropriate to industry (assessment offer, research report, etc.)

## STEP 5: Pack Summary
Present the completed pack with:
- Stories sourced and used
- Assets produced
- Gaps where more engagement data is needed
- Recommended next step (publish / strengthen with more client stories / run ABM campaign)

## Rules
- Separate Axelerant-derived insight from external research clearly — label each source
- Remove client names from all outputs
- Do not fabricate industry data — cite sources for all external claims
- If not enough engagement data exists for this vertical, flag it explicitly rather than padding content
