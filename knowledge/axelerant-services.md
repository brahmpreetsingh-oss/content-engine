## Industry Model

**Axelerant is industry agnostic.** The team does not target a fixed set of verticals. Industry content is produced for industries where Axelerant actually wins clients — content maps to real won accounts, not to a predefined list.

**How the Content Engine handles industry:**
- When a story comes from an engagement in a specific industry, the Story Intelligence Agent captures the industry context from the evidence (meeting notes, deal records, delivery documents)
- The Story Classifier maps the story to the actual industry of the client — not to a generic category
- Industry-angle content is produced from real won engagements, grounded in what actually happened in that sector
- No industry is pre-excluded or pre-prioritized — the pipeline follows the evidence

**Industry angle content** is written when the engagement lesson connects meaningfully to broader patterns in that industry — compliance pressures, buyer behavior, technology adoption curves, regulatory context, or organizational dynamics specific to that sector.

**What this means in practice**: If Axelerant wins a project with a healthcare association, a higher education university, and a B2B SaaS company in the same quarter — the Content Engine produces industry-angle content for all three, because the evidence exists. If only healthcare wins are active, only healthcare content is produced.
