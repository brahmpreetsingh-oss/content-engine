# Channel Asset Templates

Copy templates for all distribution asset types. Use these as structural scaffolding — adapt the content to the specific story and tier. Never use template filler text in final copy.

---

## Asset 1 — LinkedIn Post (Primary)

```
LINKEDIN POST (PRIMARY)
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

[HOOK — 1-2 lines. Specific claim, outcome, or question. No company name. No "excited to share."]

[LINE BREAK]

[CONTEXT — 2-3 sentences. What was the situation? Why does it matter to this reader?]

[INSIGHT — The core lesson or approach. Be specific enough that a practitioner recognizes it as real. 3-5 short paragraphs or structured lines.]

[LINE BREAK]

[REFERENCE — One sentence pointing to the full blog with a specific reason to click.]
→ [blog URL]

[HASHTAGS: #[platform] #[practice-area] #[industry] #Axelerant]

══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Word count target: 150–300 words
```

## Asset 3 — Newsletter Blurb

```
NEWSLETTER BLURB
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

[HEADLINE — punchy, outcome-forward, 10 words max]

[1-2 sentences: what is this piece and why should the reader care? Not a summary — a value proposition.]

[1 sentence: who is this most relevant for?]

[LINK TEXT → blog URL]

══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Word count target: 80–120 words
Tone: Warmer, more conversational than LinkedIn. You are writing for a subscriber who already trusts you.
```

## Asset 4 — Sales Enablement Summary

```
SALES ENABLEMENT SUMMARY
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
USE WHEN: Your prospect is dealing with [specific challenge or trigger context]. Share this when discussing [platform or service area].

PROSPECT CONTEXT: [1-2 sentences on what kind of buyer this is most relevant for — industry, role, deal stage]

THE STORY IN ONE PARAGRAPH:
[100-150 words. Plain language. Outcome-first. Describe the challenge, the approach, and the result in a way that makes a prospect think "that's exactly what we're dealing with."]

WHY IT MATTERS TO YOUR PROSPECT:
[2-3 sentences connecting the story directly to a prospect's likely pain points or evaluation criteria.]

SUGGESTED USE IN OUTREACH:
[1-2 sentence script or framing the BDR can use to introduce the piece in a cold email, follow-up, or call.]

→ Full article: [blog URL]

══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Word count target: 200–300 words
Audience: BDR / Account Executive using this in prospect outreach
```

## Asset 5 — Campaign Snippet

```
CAMPAIGN SNIPPET
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
[HEADLINE — 70 characters max. Outcome-first. No "we" or company name.]

[BODY — 2-3 sentences. 150 characters max. Specific enough to earn a click. Answer: why should this person read this now?]

CTA: [Read the story →] / [See the breakdown →] / [Learn how →]

══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Word count target: 40–60 words
Use: Email campaign body or LinkedIn sponsored content
Note: A/B test this against the alternative LinkedIn hook for ad performance.
```

## Asset 6 — Internal Win Announcement

```
INTERNAL WIN ANNOUNCEMENT
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Hey team 👋

[1-2 sentences: what is the story about and why does it matter to Axelerant?]

[1-2 sentences: specific callout of the team or individuals involved — name them if appropriate.]

[1 sentence: what the publication means externally — proof point, visibility, platform partnership, etc.]

→ [blog URL]

Go ahead and share if this resonates with you — the more it reaches the right audience, the better.

══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Word count target: 100–150 words
Tone: Warm, proud, collegial. Never corporate. Reads like it was written by a colleague, not marketing.
Channels: Slack #announcements or equivalent internal channel
```

## Asset 7 — Platform POC Brief

```
PLATFORM POC BRIEF
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Platform: [HubSpot / Drupal / Acquia / AWS]
Contact: [Partner contact name/role if known]

HI [Name],

We've just published a piece that we think could be valuable co-marketing material for [platform name].

THE STORY:
[2-3 sentences. What did Axelerant and [platform] do together? What was the result?]

HOW [PLATFORM] CONTRIBUTED:
[2-3 sentences. Be specific about how the platform's capabilities, support, or certification network contributed to the outcome. This is the partner's hook — make it visible.]

WHAT WE'RE SUGGESTING:
[1-2 sentences. How could the partner share this? Which of their channels is the best fit?]

→ Full article: [blog URL]

Happy to discuss a formal co-marketing amplification if there's interest.

══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Word count target: 150–200 words
Audience: HubSpot / Drupal / Acquia / AWS partner contact or co-marketing team
Tone: Professional, partnership-focused, not sales-y
```
