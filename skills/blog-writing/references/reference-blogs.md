## How to Use These References

When writing a new blog, fetch 1-2 of the most relevant reference blogs and analyze:

1. **Opening hook style**: How does the piece start? What draws the reader in without being generic?
2. **Section transitions**: How does the narrative flow from problem → approach → outcome?
3. **Technical specificity level**: How much implementation detail is included? At what depth?
4. **Paragraph density**: How long are paragraphs? How much is explained vs. implied?
5. **Tone calibration**: How formal or conversational is the voice? How is Axelerant positioned?
6. **CTA style**: How does the piece close? What action is the reader invited to take?

Use these observations to calibrate the draft — the goal is a piece that fits naturally alongside these references on the Axelerant blog.
